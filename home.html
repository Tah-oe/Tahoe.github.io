Where Health Meets Happiness.

Hawa 180 is more than just a gym; it's a movement dedicated to empowering women to embrace their strength, beauty, and resilience. Join us on this transformative journey, where every step forward is a celebration of the extraordinary power within each woman. Welcome to Hawa 180 – where your journey to a healthy life-style begins.

Life at the GYM

At Hawa 180, our aim is simple yet profound — to empower women on their unique journeys to wellness. We are more than just a gym; we are a sanctuary where strength meets femininity, and where every woman is celebrated for her individuality. Our mission is to foster a supportive community, offering personalized fitness guidance, inspiring classes, and a space where women can embrace their strength, resilience, and well-being.
